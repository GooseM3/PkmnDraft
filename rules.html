Rules & Regulations of the 2024 Season

On this page, you can find all info on the official Rules & Regulations that apply at the VGC events of the 2024 Season. These rules apply to all events, regardless of the entity hosting them, and also apply to community-run grassroots events.

Distinctions

  • Battle rules: General set of rules that apply to all battles, such as all matches being Double Battles played with a game time of 20 minutes.
  • Team rules: General set of rules that apply to all teams, such as all Pokémon being auto-leveled to level 50, or the Species and Item Clauses.
  • Regulations: Specific set of rules that apply on top of the previous two. These refer to specific Pokémon, moves, or items that are allowed or banned, and change during the season.

Battle Rules

  • Players will play in Double Battles in Pokémon Scarlet and Pokémon Violet.
  • Each player selects four out of the six Pokémon in their Battle Team.
  • Gameplay continues until a player makes all four of their opponent’s Pokémon faint or until the in-game time limit has expired.

Team Rules

  • Players may use Pokémon that were obtained in Pokémon Scarlet or Pokémon Violet, imported into Pokémon Scarlet or Pokémon Violet via Pokémon HOME, or received through an official event or distribution.
  • Pokémon may make use of any move or Ability available to that Pokémon through normal gameplay.

Format Rotations

All official events for the 2024 season will use Pokémon Scarlet & Pokémon Violet until further notice. This applies to TPCI, TPC, and TPCK events.
